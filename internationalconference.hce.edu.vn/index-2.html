The University of Economics, Hue University (HUE), is set to to host the International Conference on “Economic and Business development in the New Era” on June 25th 2025, in Hue city, Vietnam.

The goal of the conference on "Economic and Business Development in the New Era" is to provide a scholarly platform for experts, researchers, entrepreneurs, and policymakers to share insights on emerging trends, opportunities, and challenges in the current situation.

Beyond offering updates on knowledge and practical experiences from leading experts, the conference also serves as a networking opportunity for organizations, businesses, and governmental agencies.

Through these discussions, stakeholders can propose strategic solutions to enhance business competitiveness, adapt to market dynamics, and optimize business models in the new era.
